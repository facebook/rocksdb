Reduced copying when reading wide-column entities whose columns reference blob files. On the point-lookup (`GetEntity`/`MultiGetEntity`) and blob-backed memtable/direct-write read paths, resolving blob-referenced columns no longer re-serializes the whole entity into a fresh buffer: resolved blob values are referenced in place from their fetched buffers. This is enabled by an internal change to `PinnableWideColumns` that lets its columns reference multiple backing buffers.
